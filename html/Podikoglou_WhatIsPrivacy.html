What is Privacy?

Defining Privacy: A Fundamental Right

Privacy is your right to control your personal information and decide how it's used. It's about safeguarding your personal space, ensuring your data isn't exploited without your consent. Recognized globally, privacy is essential for maintaining human dignity and freedom.

The Evolving Concept of Online Privacy

In today's digital world, online privacy has transformed. Initially, the internet was about sharing information openly, but now, with data collection and tracking, protecting your personal information online is more crucial than ever. Understanding how your data is used and taking steps to protect it is key to maintaining your privacy in the digital age.
